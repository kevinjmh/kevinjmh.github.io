command_type 命令类型：
0 启动工作流,
1 从当前节点开始执行,
2 恢复被容错的工作流,
3 恢复暂停流程,
4 从失败节点开始执行,
5 补数,
6 调度,
7 重跑,
8 暂停,
9 停止,
10 恢复等待线程

select id,name,process_definition_id,state,
timediff(end_time,start_time) as time_cost,
host,worker_group
from dolphinscheduler.t_ds_process_instance
where end_time is not null
order by
start_time desc limit 10
